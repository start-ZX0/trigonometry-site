To find Tangent:

tan(θ) = sin(θ) / cos(θ)

To find Sine:

sin(θ) = tan(θ) × cos(θ)

To find Cosine:

cos(θ) = sin(θ) / tan(θ)

sin = المقابل / الوتر
cos = المجاور / الوتر
tan = sin / cos
csc = 1 / sin
sec = 1 / cos
cot = 1 / tan
